GoodPlatform.org is coming soon

Two years ago we launched a service called GoodPlatform.org focused on the intersection of activism and social media. Within three months of launch we had close to 30,000 active users, thousands of shares, and the community helped donate hundreds to nonprofits around the world.

We put the project on hold due to the uncertainty of the political landscape, and the looming fallout of bots, foreign cyber attacks, and the egregious Facebook/Cambridge Analytica privacy attack on society.

We knew that if we were to relaunch we would need to release a service that is safe, secure, user-focused, and decent privacy protections built right in.

Sign up for the relaunch beta.

The last year we have been putting the finishing touches on the design, the platform, data portability, and a new system of "power-ups" to super charge your posts and keep the service, free - without advertisements or selling your data.
